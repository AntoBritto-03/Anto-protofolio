Space Data Exploration

Project Description

This project ventures into the unknown frontiers of space exploration using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) with Power BI. By visualizing and analyzing space mission data, we'll uncover hidden trends in launch activity, success rates, and rocket status (whether active or retired). Our goal isn't just to chronicle past achievements, but to identify patterns between budgets, launch locations, rocket types, companies, and mission outcomes. This in-depth analysis holds the potential to unlock valuable knowledge, optimizing future missions and maximizing the return on investment in humanity's grand quest to explore the cosmos.
